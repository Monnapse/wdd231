GAME CONCEPT

WHAT IS SHIFTWAY?

A fast-paced FPS where gravity is a weapon and every surface is a battlefield. The game introduces a new take on movement-based shooters, requiring 360-degree spatial awareness.The gravity-shifting mechanics and unique movement system create a dynamic and disorienting experience. As players move through the station, gravity can be manipulated, allowing them to walk along walls or the ceiling as if they're simply walking on the floor. With the push of a button or a clever use of a gadget or jetpack, players can flip gravity to seamlessly transition between surfaces, adding layers of strategy and exploration. Each new room or hallway offers a fresh perspective, where the usual concept of "up" and "down" no longer applies. This opens up creative possibilities for combat and navigation, requiring players to constantly adjust their spatial awareness as they encounter new challenges in this ever-shifting environment.

WHY IT’S UNIQUE?

Unlike traditional FPS games, there is no fixed ground players move across walls, ceilings, and floating platforms. The challenge isn't just shooting it's navigating and mastering the shifting gravity mechanics.
